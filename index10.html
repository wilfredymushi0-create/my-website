Scrambled Eggs

✨ creamy • fluffy • pure comfort ✨

🍳 Eggs are one of my favourite foods. Here is a recipe for deliciously rich scrambled eggs.

🥣 Ingredients
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 tbs butter
  • 2 tbs cream
👩‍🍳 Method
  1. Melt butter in a frying pan over a medium heat
  2. Gently mix the eggs and cream in a bowl
  3. Once butter has melted add cream and eggs
  4. Using a spatula fold the eggs from the edge of the pan to the center every 20 seconds
  5. When the eggs are still moist remove from the heat
🔑

Chef's tip: For extra creamy eggs, remove them from the heat just before they look fully set — residual heat works wonders!

⏲️ ready in 5 minutes · serves 1-2
